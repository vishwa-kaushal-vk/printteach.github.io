F560 - 60si CIJ Inkjet Printer

High-Definition Precision Printer Equipped with a precision 60μm nozzle, the F560-60si is optimized for applications requiring fine, detailed characters and high-definition codes. It supports higher print speeds (up to 520m/min) and a larger 34-dot matrix, allowing for more complex multi-line messages compared to the F540. This model is ideal for marking smaller products or high-density information where clarity is critical.
