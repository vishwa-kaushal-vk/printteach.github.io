F680 CIJ Inkjet Printer

High-Contrast Cable & Wire Printer Built to withstand harsh industrial environments, the F680 excels in high-temperature and high-humidity conditions common in cable manufacturing. It utilizes high-adhesion heavy pigment white ink to produce bright, highly visible markings on dark surfaces like power cables and automotive wiring. Features like specific cable-industry functions ensure long-lasting legibility and superior recognition efficiency.
